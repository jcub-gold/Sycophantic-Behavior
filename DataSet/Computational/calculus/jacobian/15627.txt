Problem:
Find the jacobian of the vector valued function $\begin{pmatrix} f(x,y,z)\\ g(x,y,z) \\ h(x,y,z)\end{pmatrix}$ where $f(x,y,z) = \sin \left(x^3 y^2\right)$, $g(x,y,z) = \tan ^{-1}\left(y^2\right)$, and $h(x,y,z) = z^2$
Answer:
$\left(\begin{array}{ccc} 3 x^2 y^2 \cos \left(x^3 y^2\right) & 2 x^3 y \cos \left(x^3 y^2\right) & 0 \\ 0 & \frac{2 y}{y^4+1} & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 2 z \\\end{array}\right)$
