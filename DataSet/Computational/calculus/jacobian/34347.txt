Problem:
Find the jacobian of the vector valued function $\begin{pmatrix} f(x,y,z)\\ g(x,y,z) \\ h(x,y,z)\end{pmatrix}$ where $f(x,y,z) = x$, $g(x,y,z) = \log (y)$, and $h(x,y,z) = \cos \left(\frac{y}{z}\right)$
Answer:
$\left(\begin{array}{ccc} 1 & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & \frac{1}{y} & 0 \\ 0 & -\frac{\sin \left(\frac{y}{z}\right)}{z} & \frac{y \sin \left(\frac{y}{z}\right)}{z^2} \\\end{array}\right)$
