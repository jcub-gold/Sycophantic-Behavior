Problem:
Find the jacobian of the vector valued function $\begin{pmatrix} f(x,y,z)\\ g(x,y,z) \\ h(x,y,z)\end{pmatrix}$ where $f(x,y,z) = \cos (x)$, $g(x,y,z) = z$, and $h(x,y,z) = \sqrt[3]{z}$
Answer:
$\left(\begin{array}{ccc} -\sin (x) & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 \\ 0 & 0 & \frac{1}{3 \sqrt[3]{z}^2} \\\end{array}\right)$
