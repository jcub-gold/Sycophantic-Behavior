Problem:
Find the curl of the vector field $f(x,y,z)\uvec{i} + g(x,y,z)\uvec{j} + h(x,y,z)\uvec{k}$ where $f(x,y,z) = \cos (y)$, $g(x,y,z) = \sqrt{\frac{x}{y}}$, and $h(x,y,z) = \sqrt{\frac{x}{z}}$
Answer:
$\left\{0,-\frac{1}{2 z \sqrt{\frac{x}{z}}},\frac{1}{2 y \sqrt{\frac{x}{y}}}+\sin (y)\right\}$
