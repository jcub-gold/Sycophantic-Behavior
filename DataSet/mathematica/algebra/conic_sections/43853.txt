Problem:
Given the equation $8 x^2-6 x-7 y^2-3 y+9=0$, rewrite and classify the conic and state relevant properties.
Answer:
Classification: Hyperbola
Equation: $8 \left(x-\frac{3}{8}\right)^2-7 \left(y+\frac{3}{14}\right)^2=-\frac{459}{56}$
Foci: $\left(
\begin{array}{cc}
 \frac{3}{8} & -\frac{3}{56} \left(4+3 \sqrt{85}\right) \\
 \frac{3}{8} & \frac{3}{56} \left(3 \sqrt{85}-4\right) \\
\end{array}
\right)$
Eccentricity: $\frac{\sqrt{\frac{15}{2}}}{2}$
Center: $\left\{\frac{3}{8},\frac{1}{2} \left(\frac{3}{56} \left(3 \sqrt{85}-4\right)-\frac{3}{56} \left(4+3 \sqrt{85}\right)\right)\right\}$
Asymptotes: $\left\{y=\frac{3}{28} \left(\sqrt{14}-2\right)-2 \sqrt{\frac{2}{7}} x,y=2 \sqrt{\frac{2}{7}} x-\frac{3}{28} \left(2+\sqrt{14}\right)\right\}$
