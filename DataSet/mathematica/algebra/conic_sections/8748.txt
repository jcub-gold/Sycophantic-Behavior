Problem:
Given the equation $9 x^2-9 x-2 y^2+y-4=0$, rewrite and classify the conic and state relevant properties.
Answer:
Classification: Hyperbola
Equation: $9 \left(x-\frac{1}{2}\right)^2-2 \left(y-\frac{1}{4}\right)^2=\frac{49}{8}$
Foci: $\left(
\begin{array}{cc}
 \frac{1}{12} \left(6-7 \sqrt{11}\right) & \frac{1}{4} \\
 \frac{1}{12} \left(6+7 \sqrt{11}\right) & \frac{1}{4} \\
\end{array}
\right)$
Eccentricity: $\sqrt{\frac{11}{2}}$
Center: $\left\{\frac{1}{2} \left(\frac{1}{12} \left(6-7 \sqrt{11}\right)+\frac{1}{12} \left(6+7 \sqrt{11}\right)\right),\frac{1}{4}\right\}$
Asymptotes: $\left\{y=\frac{3 x}{\sqrt{2}}+\frac{1}{4} \left(1-3 \sqrt{2}\right),y=\frac{1}{4} \left(1+3 \sqrt{2}\right)-\frac{3 x}{\sqrt{2}}\right\}$
